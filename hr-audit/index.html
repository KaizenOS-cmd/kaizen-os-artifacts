Quick Wins

3 Governance Fixes You Can Ship This Week

You don't need a transformation to start fixing this. You need three decisions.

Quick Win 01

Create a Single Intake Point

The fix: designate one channel as the official HRIS request intake. Everything else gets redirected to it.

How to do it in 48 hours

  1. Pick your tool: JSM, ServiceNow, a Notion form, or even a Google Form.
  2. Create one request category: HRIS Change Request.
  3. Set an auto-reply on your inbox: "All HRIS requests must be submitted via the intake form. Requests received here will not be actioned."
  4. Communicate it to HR, Payroll, and IT in one message.

Why it works: 80% of backlog chaos comes from ungoverned intake. One channel eliminates the noise immediately.

⏱️ 2–3 hours to implement
📈 Immediate reduction in reactive work
Quick Win 02

Name an Owner for Every Active Integration

The fix: pull a list of every active HRIS integration. Assign a named human owner to each one today.

How to do it in 48 hours

  1. Export your active integration list from your HRIS (Workday, JSM, etc.).
  2. Create a simple table: Integration | System | Owner | Last Reviewed.
  3. Assign an owner to every row — not a team, a person.
  4. Set a calendar reminder to review the list quarterly.

Why it works: Integrations are your highest-risk failure points. When they break with no owner, the incident becomes everyone's emergency and no one's responsibility.

⏱️ 1–2 hours to implement
📈 Eliminates ownership ambiguity on your riskiest dependencies
Quick Win 03

Log the Next 5 Configuration Changes Before They Happen

The fix: before your next 5 HRIS changes go live, document them in a decision log. Build the habit now.

Your decision log needs 5 fields

  1. Change description
  2. Business reason
  3. Who requested it
  4. Who approved it
  5. Date implemented

Why it works: Configuration drift is the silent killer of HRIS integrity. A decision log gives you an audit trail, reduces rework, and protects you when something breaks.

⏱️ 30 minutes to set up
📈 Instant audit readiness on every change
